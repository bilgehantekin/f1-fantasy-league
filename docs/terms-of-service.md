**Prediction Submission**:
- Predictions are locked at the official race start time
- Late submissions are NOT accepted
- Edits are NOT allowed after submission
- Your predictions must be original (no cheating/collusion)

**Scoring System**:
- Scores calculated using official Formula 1 race results (OpenF1 API)
- Points awarded based on accuracy of predictions
- Joker cards (if available) double points for that prediction
- Final standings determined automatically

**Disputes**:
- Scoring is final and determined by the system
- We do not overturn scores except for technical errors
- Contact support if you believe there's a technical error

### 5.3 Joker Cards

**What Are Joker Cards?**
- Special power-ups that double points for one prediction
- Available to premium members or as seasonal rewards
- One joker card per race (if available)
- Once used, cannot be recovered

**Usage**:
- Jokers can only be used before prediction lock
- Used automatically when prediction is submitted (if selected)
- Refunds are NOT issued for joker card usage

In leagues, you must:
