## 5. League Rules & Gameplay

### 5.1 Creating & Managing Leagues

**League Owner Responsibilities**:
- You must be 18+ years old to create a league
- You are responsible for league rules and member conduct
- You can remove members at your discretion
- You can transfer league ownership or delete the league

**League Invitations**:
- Invite codes are unique per league
- Sharing the code = giving access (treat as confidential)
- Users who join agree to these Terms and the league's rules

### 5.2 Predictions & Scoring

**Prediction Submission**:
- Predictions are locked at the official race start time
- Late submissions are NOT accepted
- Edits are NOT allowed after submission
- Your predictions must be original (no cheating/collusion)

**Scoring System**:
- Scores are calculated using race results sourced from OpenF1 and other publicly available, unofficial third-party feeds
- Points awarded based on accuracy of predictions
- Joker cards (if available) double points for that prediction
- Final standings determined automatically

**Disputes**:
- Scoring is final and determined by the system
- We do not overturn scores except for technical errors
- Contact support if you believe there's a technical error

### 5.3 Joker Question

**What Is the Joker?**
- A bonus question attached to some races (e.g., "Will there be a safety car?") worth extra points if you answer correctly.
- The joker question only becomes visible inside the prediction screen during the **24 hours before prediction lock** for that race; before that window it is hidden for everyone equally.
- Joker availability and points value can vary per race and may not exist for every race.

**Usage**:
- The joker can only be answered before prediction lock.
- If you do not answer, you simply forgo the bonus points; nothing is consumed.
- Joker answers, like other predictions, can be cleared from the prediction screen until lock.

### 5.4 League Conduct

In leagues, you must:
- Treat other members with respect
- Not spam or abuse other members
- Not coordinate to gain unfair advantage
- Report abuse to league owner or us
