# GridCall Support

Need help with GridCall? You're in the right place.

## Quick Help

**Can't sign in?**
Make sure you're using the email you registered with. If you've forgotten your password, tap **Forgot password?** on the sign-in screen and we'll email you a reset link.

**Didn't get a verification or reset email?**
Check your spam folder. If it's still missing after a few minutes, contact us.

**Predictions not saving?**
Predictions lock at the start of each session (qualifying, sprint, or race). Once a session is locked, you can't change your picks. Make sure you have an active internet connection and try again.

**Notifications aren't working?**
Open **Profile → Notification Settings** and check that reminders are enabled. Then verify GridCall has notification permission in iOS Settings.

**Want to delete your account?**
Open **Profile → Account → Delete account**. This permanently removes your profile, predictions, and league memberships.

**Premium subscription questions?**
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions** on your iPhone to view, cancel, or change your plan. Restore a previous purchase from **Profile → Restore purchases**.
