# GridCall Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 9 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GridCall ("we", "us", or "the app"), an independent F1 prediction and fantasy league app developed by Bilgehan Tekin, collects, uses, and protects your information.

By using GridCall, you agree to the practices described below.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
- **Account information** — email address, username, and password (passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text).
- **Profile information** — username, optional notification preferences, language preference.
- **Predictions and league activity** — picks you make for each race, leagues you create or join, invite codes you use.

### Information collected automatically
- **Device and technical data** — basic information needed for the app to function: iOS version, device model, app version, anonymous device identifier (used for push notifications).
- **Usage and crash data** — anonymous error reports and crash logs (no personal user identifier is attached).

### Information we do **not** collect
- We do not collect your real name, address, phone number, or payment information.
- We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or location.
- We do not sell your data to anyone.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
- Show your predictions, scores, and league standings.
- Send you optional reminders and weekly summary notifications (only if you enable them).
- Improve app stability by analyzing anonymous crash reports.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Third-Party Services

GridCall relies on the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy.

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Backend, authentication, database | Email, username, predictions, league data |
| **Apple Push Notification Service** | Push notifications | Anonymous device token |
| **RevenueCat** | Premium subscription management | Anonymous purchase identifier (no personal info) |
| **Sentry** | Anonymous crash reporting | Crash diagnostics (no personal user identifier) |

We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.
- We do not store payment card information; subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data — visible inside the app (profile, predictions, leagues).
- **Update** your information — edit your username, notification preferences, and language at any time.
- **Delete** your account — open **Profile → Account → Delete account** in the app. This permanently removes your profile, predictions, and league memberships.
- **Export** or request a copy of your data — email us using the address below.

If you are in the European Union, United Kingdom, or another region with data protection laws (GDPR, UK GDPR, KVKK in Türkiye), you also have the right to object to processing, restrict processing, or lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 6. Children

GridCall is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

## 7. Subscriptions

GridCall Premium subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not see or store your payment details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in **iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions**.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted in the app or sent to your registered email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

## 9. Independent Fan App
