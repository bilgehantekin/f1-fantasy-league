# GridCall — F1 Predictions With Your Friends

**Predict the race. Beat your friends. Climb the leaderboard.**

GridCall turns every F1 weekend into a friendly competition. Create a private league, invite your friends with a code, lock in your predictions before the lights go out, and watch the standings update as soon as the chequered flag drops.

## Why GridCall

- **Private leagues** — Race only against the people you invite. No public noise.
- **Real F1 calendar** — Every Grand Prix and sprint weekend, including session times in your local timezone.
- **Smart predictions** — Pick the podium, pole, fastest lap, DNFs, safety car, and more.
- **Weekly summaries** — A clean recap of every weekend, with shareable cards for your group chat.
- **Season-long standings** — Track who's actually the best tipster across the whole year.
- **Reminders** — Optional notifications before predictions lock, so you never miss a session.
- **Türkçe ve English** — Full localization for both languages.

## How It Works

1. **Sign up** in seconds with email or Apple.
2. **Create a league** or **join one** with an invite code.
3. **Make your predictions** before each session locks.
4. **Compare results** as soon as the race ends.
5. **Climb the standings** week by week.

## GridCall Premium

Take your league to the next level with **GridCall Premium**:
- Join up to **10 active leagues** (free accounts: 2)
- **Detailed league stats** — totals, ranks, averages, best weekend, season trend
- **Favorite leagues** pinned to the top
- A clean **Premium badge** on your profile and standings

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. Cancel anytime in iOS Settings.
